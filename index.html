Welcome to Wombourne Tennis Club

Wombourne Tennis Club is a section of the Wombourne Cricket, Tennis & Bowls Club situated on the green in the centre of the village. The tennis section has existed for over 50 years, ever since the grounds were donated to the club for the playing of sport and boasts an ever growing number of members.

The main clubhouse is used by the Tennis, Cricket and Bowls clubs and is host to a number of events throughout the year, such as cabarets, quizzes and dances. Our section is organised by a committee who are elected each year at our annual general meeting.

We have two teams for both the Men's and Ladies in the Staffordshire Inter Tennis League and a Ladies and Mixed team in the Hereford & Worcester Winter League. Throughout the year, the tennis section hosts a large number of tournaments and events which all members are encouraged to join. Juniors tennis and coaching sessions for a variety of ages are also available.
