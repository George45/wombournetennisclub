Ken Roberts Cup

The Ken Roberts Cup is one of our most popular events of the year often featuring a large number of competitors from the tennis club. The tournament takes place during the annual Wombourne carnival which helps to ensure a lively and energetic atmosphere around the village.

This cup was provided by the Tennis Section is honour of former member, Ken Roberts, who was a long-serving and enthusiastic player. The cup is presented to the winners of the annual tournament, usually by his wife Elaine.

Ken was a member for many years who contributed significantly, not only by playing in the men's team, but also by arranging and carrying out the coaching of many of the section's junior players. There are still several, now senior, players in the section who will forever be grateful for Ken's assistance.
